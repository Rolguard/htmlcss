Overview

Heyo guys! I'm Andrew Sugito, a 2nd year student studying Commerce & Computer Science at the University of New South Wales (UNSW).


I graduated from Sydney Technical High School in 2020 and participated in various activities throughout high school. I played volleyball knockouts and other tournaments such as the Schools Cup for my high school team as an opposite (R.I.P Carlos).
